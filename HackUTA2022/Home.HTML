As a group of first year students that commute to school daily, we have seen first hand how finding parking on campus is a struggle. Of course this is the first year since the COVID-19 quarantine where almost all students are attending classes in person. The school has implemented solutions with a price and we strive to provide a solution that does not impact anyone economically. We present a solution that will save hours on end for the collective student body.


How it Works

Our website and app will allow students to put in their destination, for example Nedderman Hall, and find the closest parking lot + real-time status for students. The status will consist of how full in capacity the parking lot is and sending notifications when a availabily opens up (Twilio).


The website and app will show the parking lots in order of most availability with both list and map interfaces. The parking lots will be color coded for quick and effecient decicion making.


Development

We currently can only show how it would work in theory by using fixed data. We used a random nunmber generator to get fixed data for each of the lots. This will show the number of amount of cars in a lot assoicating with their corresponding lot capacity. The data determines the color based on percantage capacity. We would require hardware since it can collect real time data. Our hardware would be servers and sensors to calculate the input and output data of the parking lots. Altough our solution to the parking time was originally designed for UTA campus students, we are sure that with some modification we can spread this concept to all industries.
